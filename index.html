Bio

I am currently a Ph.D. student in College of Information Sciences and Technology at Penn State (IST@PSU). I am a member of Visual Data Research group since Aug. 2015. My advisor is Prof. James Z. Wang. Previously, I received a BS.c. in Statistics from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), 2015.

My research interests are in Computer Vision, Data Mining and Machine learning. My recent focus is a Computer Vision approach in the satellite storm prediction, which is a real-life application of machine learning algorithm. My other projects involve statistical inference and signal processing.
